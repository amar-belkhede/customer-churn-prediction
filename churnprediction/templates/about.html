What is churn and why is it important? 💰

Churn measures a company's loss in subscribers within a given period of time. Churn rates are applied to subscription-based businesses such as Telecommunications, OTT (Over The Top) streaming servicies, DTH (Direct To Home) providers, Cloud Computing services, etc.

Churn rate helps businesses to identify the steps or stages when a customer will leave a service. It takes companies 6 times more investment in getting new customers than keeping their current customers. This is why analysis of Churn is an important business 💵 problem.

About the dataset 📊

We have used IBM Telecom company dataset for this project which is publicly available on kaggle. The dataset contains 7043 entries and 21 features. The label contains 'Yes' and 'No' values denoting whether the customer will churn or not. It is a tabular dataset with size of 955KB.

How to use the application? 👨‍💻

Simply give input to our app and it'll predict whether the customer will churn or not. Refer analysis sheet to know which variables affect churn more.
